About BSDC

Bangladesh Software Development Community — Building Bangladesh's tech future, one developer at a time.

Our Mission

BSDC exists to empower every software developer in Bangladesh with the knowledge, connections, and tools they need to build world-class technology. We believe that Bangladeshi developers are among the most talented in the world, and they deserve a platform that matches their ambition.

Our platform brings together developers from Dhaka, Chittagong, Sylhet, Rajshahi, Khulna, Barisal, Mymensingh, Rangpur, and every corner of Bangladesh — uniting them in a single, vibrant community of knowledge sharing and collaboration.

What Makes BSDC Different

Bangladesh-First

Built specifically for Bangladeshi developers, with support for both English and Bengali content, local context, and Bangladesh-specific technical topics.

Meet the Founder

R

Rizwan Rahim Chowdhury

Founder & Lead Developer, BSDC

Founder Lead Developer Software Engineer

Rizwan Rahim Chowdhury is the visionary founder and lead developer of the Bangladesh Software Development Community (BSDC). A passionate software engineer and community builder, Rizwan created BSDC with a clear mission: to give Bangladesh's rapidly growing developer community the world-class platform they deserve.

With a deep understanding of both the technical challenges facing Bangladeshi developers and the gaps in existing global platforms, Rizwan designed BSDC from the ground up to solve real problems faced by developers across Bangladesh — from students in university computer science programs to senior engineers at top Bangladeshi tech companies.

Rizwan believes that open knowledge sharing is the foundation of a thriving tech ecosystem. By building BSDC as a free, accessible platform, he hopes to accelerate Bangladesh's journey to becoming a global technology powerhouse — nurturing the next generation of world-class software developers.
